# Brag Plan: AI Folders

## What is this app?
A free, open-source browser extension that organizes your AI conversations into
folders and gives you a reusable prompt library — across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini,
Copilot, DeepSeek, Grok, Perplexity, and your own local LLM. One tool, every AI.

## The angle
Every AI chat lives in its own siloed, ever-growing, unsearchable list. AI Folders
is the one layer that sits on top of *all* of them: folders for your chats, a prompt
library you can fire into any chat box with `#name` + Space, synced everywhere. The
through-line is the product's own signature brand gradient — green → teal → blue →
violet → orange — which is literally the colors of the AIs it unifies (ChatGPT green,
Gemini blue, Claude orange). The video treats a genuinely useful free tool like the
premium product it is.

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)
The pain, shown not told: a long, flat, ungrouped list of AI conversations scrolling
past — "New chat", "New chat", "Untitled". One line: **"Your AI chats. One endless list."**

## Key moments (the middle)
- The popup glides in and **folders snap into place one by one** with emoji labels
  (📁 Work, 📁 Research, 📁 Recipes, 📁 Code) — the messy list becomes order.
- The **prompt library**: switch to 📝 mode, then `#review` + Space **types the saved
  prompt straight into a chat box** — the one-of-a-kind feature, shown as a real action.
- **Every AI**: the seventeen service logos arrive one by one along the brand gradient (two rows),
  plus a local-LLM chip. "One library. Every AI."

## Outro / punchline
The wordmark lands in the brand gradient. Tagline: **"Organize your AI conversations."**
Trust chips — Free · Open source · Private · 43 languages — then `aifolders.xyz`.

## User flow worth showing
entry → key action → result, twice:
1. Open popup over a messy chat list → folders organize the conversations (result: order).
2. In a chat box, type `#review` + Space → the stored prompt is injected (result: instant prompt).
These two are the centerpiece scenes (Scene 2 and Scene 3).

- Role: warm, clean corporate bed with a consistent light SFX layer (app-store).
- Music: `happy-beats-business-moves-vol-1-by-ende-dot-app.mp3` at ~0.32.
- Music treatment: in from 0s, hold under the whole edit, gentle duck/fade under the
  final logo so the bell rings.
- Music cue guidance: bundled preset (120.19 BPM). Strong cues cluster 16–24s; lock the
  **outro logo** to **17.02s**. Beat grid ~0.5s — use it for the folder and logo reveals.
- Audio-reactive treatment: subtle; let the brand-gradient glow / logo presence breathe
  with RMS. No waveform/equalizer visuals.
- SFX posture: moderate, motion-matched — soft drops for folders, keyboard ticks for the
  typed prompt, soft card/drop for logo arrivals, one bell on the outro.

- Restraint rule: no sound stacking, nothing aggressive; all SFX 0.5–0.75.

## Storyboard

### Scene 1 — The pile — 3.0s
A faithful AI-chat sidebar: a flat, ungrouped list of ~8 conversation rows
("New chat", "Untitled", repeated) under a search bar. The list drifts upward. Line
fades up centered: **"Your AI chats. One endless list."** Hold to read.
Sequential/interaction: none (ambient drift only).
Audio intent: music establishes; quiet, slightly unresolved.
Audio-coupled idea: none.
Music: warm bed in from 0s.
Transition mood: clean crossfade → Scene 2

### Scene 2 — Folders — 4.5s
The AI Folders popup glides in (dark glass, gradient logo + 📁/📝 toggle, search). Four
folders **appear one by one** with emoji labels and chat counts: 📁 Work, 📁 Research,
📁 Recipes, 📁 Code. The pile is now order. Caption under header: **"Folders for everything."**
Sequential/interaction: yes — 4 folder rows drop in ~0.55s apart, then hold the full set.
Audio intent: each arrival feels placed and satisfying.
Audio-coupled idea: soft `drop` SFX on each folder (beat-grid ~4.0/4.6/5.2/5.8s).
Music: bed continues.
Transition mood: clean wipe → Scene 3

### Scene 3 — Prompt library + injection — 4.5s
Toggle slides 📁 → 📝. A prompt row shows **`#review`**. Below, a chat input box: a
cursor types **`#review`** then a Space, and the saved prompt text **types itself into
the box** ("Review this code for bugs, clarity, and edge cases…"). Caption:
**"Type #name. Your prompt appears."**
Sequential/interaction: yes — simulate toggle switch, then per-character typing into the box.
Audio intent: tactile, precise; the feature feels real.
Audio-coupled idea: `switch` on the toggle; randomized `keyboard` ticks during typing.
Music: bed continues.
Transition mood: clean wipe → Scene 4

### Scene 4 — Every AI — 4.0s
Seventeen real service logos arrive **one by one** along a glowing brand-gradient rail
(two rows: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini … Character.AI, Baidu) — plus a local-LLM chip.
Caption holds after the set: **"One library. Every AI."**
Sequential/interaction: yes — 7 logos pop in on the beat grid (~0.5s apart), hold full set.
Audio intent: building, additive, momentum into the outro.
Audio-coupled idea: soft `card-place` / `drop` per logo on consecutive beats.
Music: bed builds toward the strong cue.
Transition mood: soft wipe → Scene 5

### Scene 5 — Outro — 4.0s
The wordmark **AI Folders** lands in the brand gradient with the product star mark.
Tagline: **"Organize your AI conversations."** Trust chips fade up: **Free · Open source ·
Private · 43 languages.** Then **aifolders.xyz**.
Sequential/interaction: chips fade up together; logo is the hero.
Audio intent: arrival and resolution; bell rings as music ducks.
Audio-coupled idea: `impactBell_heavy_000` on the logo landing — beat-locked 17.02s.
Music: gentle duck/fade under the bell to the end.
Transition mood: hold to black.
